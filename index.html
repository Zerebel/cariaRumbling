Getting fit with Caria's wellbeign walks

We are a welcoming club with no age restrictions, and non-members are invited to come along for four or five walks before joining. Visitors are also welcome in Legon. If you need more information, see the Routes Program or contact a walk coach for specifics

On 7 May 1837 the Caria Ramblers Association was officially created. We're the only charity dedicated to removing barriers so everyone can enjoy walking in green spaces, and to improving Legon's most popular and least-known walking places.
